    About Us

    GM Sound is Now comming with a New Branch GM Event Management Company in Motihari. We provide our services in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal. We also provide a wide range of sound systems for your personal as well as professional needs. GM Sound was founded in 1994 by Kumar Sanjay and Anita Devi with a vision to make every event of Motihari district as grand as possible. This vision is embedded in the soul of the company and each member of this company works towards achieving it everyday.

    Our Mission

    Any event whether big or small holds a very special place in heart. We are dedicated to make your events embed into your memory forever. Our creative team will always provide you with unique set of ideas which suits your occasion.

    Our Plan

    We provide a wide range of plans perfectly created for all your needs. Each plan is very carefully curated by our management to make sure your events are grand and its within affordable rates. Please reach out to us for more details.

    Our Vision

    GM Sound & GM Event is committed to make every possible effort to make weddings and other events grand while keeping the essence of the occasion intact.
